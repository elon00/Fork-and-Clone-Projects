Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service.

LinkHub is a platform that allows you to create a personalized page with all your important links in one place. It's perfect for sharing on social media profiles where you can only add one link, like Instagram, Twitter, or TikTok. Instead of constantly changing that one link, you can use your LinkHub page to direct your audience to all your content.

Creating a LinkHub page is simple. Sign up for an account, choose a username (which will be your LinkHub URL), add your profile information, and start adding links. You can customize the appearance of your page with different themes and colors, and rearrange your links by dragging and dropping them in the order you prefer.

Yes, with our Business plan, you can connect your own custom domain to your LinkHub page. This gives your page a more professional look and reinforces your brand. We provide simple instructions to help you set up the domain connection through your domain registrar.

Our analytics provide insights into how your links are performing. You can see the number of views your page receives, which links are getting the most clicks, and where your visitors are coming from. Pro and Business plans offer more detailed analytics, including geographic location, device information, and referral sources.

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll immediately gain access to the additional features of your new plan. If you downgrade, you'll continue to have access to your current plan's features until the end of your billing cycle.
